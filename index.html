Welcome

Agape House is a nonprofit food bank and more! Founded in 1986 by Kathleen Knudson with the express purpose of serving those in need within the greater Hermiston area. We rely on the support of individuals, businesses, churches, and other organizations, as well as being a part of the Eastern Oregon Mission.
