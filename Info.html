Help Out With Bottle Recycling

  You can help support the Agape House by bringing in bottles and cans. These bottles and cans will be recycled either for money or used to create items used in the Agape House's projects. These items could even become utensils in shelters!

Get Help With Our Food Program

  The Agape House provides emergency food boxes for the elderly, children, single-parent households, the unemployed, newly unemployed, and the homeless. Through this program the Agape House will accept shelf stable, refigerated, and frozen items. These items can bring any family a happy night and spread our mission.
